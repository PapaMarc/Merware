The Seinfeld-Carlstrom Wedding Weekend

Plan of Events

September 25 – 27, 1998

Accommodations, Weather and other Attractions:

  • The wedding will be held at the Edgewater Hotel in downtown Seattle. The Edgewater is the only waterfront hotel in Seattle. It overlooks Elliott bay (Puget Sound) and the Olympic Mountains. You can bring a fishing pole and drop a line from your room -- the way the Beatles once did. The Seinfeld and Carlstrom families will be staying at the Edgewater. Wedding guests will receive a 10% discount on the published room rate so please mention the Seinfeld-Carlstrom wedding when making your reservation. Please make your reservation as soon as possible.

Friday, September 25

Evening:

Rehearsal Dinner Cruise

Hosted by Bob and Judy Seinfeld

 

We welcome our family and out-of-town guests to join us on a sunset dinner cruise around Lake Washington. Enjoy views of the University of Washington’s Husky Stadium, Bill Gates’ house, and Mt. Rainier (clouds permitting). Dress is casual. A light jacket is recommended.

Her majesty, Celebrations, will depart from the AGC Marina (Dock E) on Lake Union at 6:00 p.m. sharp. Please plan on arriving at the dock between 5:30 and 5:45 so you don't miss the boat! The cruise will last for approximately 3 hours. The AGC Marina is located at 1200 Westlake Avenue North.

The Edgewater concierge can order a taxi.

Driving directions:

  1. Turn left out of the Edgewater parking lot on to Alaskan Way.
  2. Turn right onto Wall Street.
  3. Turn left onto Westlake Avenue North.

 

Saturday, September 26

  • Day:

    There are many Seattle sights and restaurants to visit during the day. We will provide you with a list of recommendations why you check in to the Edgewater. Marc’s best recommendation is the Seattle Aquarium on Pier 59 (a short walk from the Edgewater) where he dives every other Thursday morning in the Pacific Coral Reef Tank to feed the fish. The weather should be in the 60s or 70s and a light jacket or rain shell is good to have.

  • Evening:

    Cocktails, ceremony and reception in the Edgewater’s Olympic Room

    Gather at 5:30 p.m. for hors d'oeuvres and cocktails while watching the sunset over the mountains. The ceremony will commence after sundown, followed by a dinner reception.

     

Sunday, September 27

10:00? a.m. to 1:00 p.m. (we can do it earlier depending on when folks have departing flights-- let us know!)

  5. Turn right onto 68th Ave NE

  9. We are at the very end of Inglewood road, which dead ends at a chain link fence. Our driveway is the second to last on the right (lakeside) at the dead end. Our house is at the bottom of the hill. Park on the street up top and walk down the driveway-- It’s a very steep, concrete, shared driveway so please be careful!
  10. You’ll pass a house on the left on your way down and if you continue walking straight you'd walk into the living room of another house 2/3 of the way down-- follow the driveway left and at the base is our home.
